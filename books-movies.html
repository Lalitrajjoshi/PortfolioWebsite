Books That Shaped My Strategy

Good to Great

Author: Jim Collins

Key Learning: The "Hedgehog Concept" helps leaders define long-term corporate strategy by aligning passion, capabilities, and economic drivers.

Thinking, Fast and Slow

Author: Daniel Kahneman

Key Learning: Understanding cognitive biases improves organizational decision-making and mitigates irrational behavior in forecasts and negotiations.

The Innovator's Dilemma

Author: Clayton M. Christensen

Key Learning: Managing disruptive innovation requires independent teams optimized for breakthrough technologies.

Movies & Business Leadership Lessons

Apollo 13

Genre: Drama / Historical

Business Lesson: Crisis management and resource scarcity highlight the value of domain expertise and calm, decentralized problem-solving.

Moneyball

Genre: Sports / Biography

Business Lesson: Data-driven strategy and talent acquisition can outperform conventional models with limited resources.
