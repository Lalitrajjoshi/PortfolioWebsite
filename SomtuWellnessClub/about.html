About the club

Wellness woven into MBA life.

SOMTU Wellness Club exists to support the academic intensity of the MBA journey with calm, vibrant, and professional wellbeing practices.

Vision and mission

Vision

To cultivate a calm, resilient, and connected MBA cohort grounded in mindful leadership.

Mission

Deliver accessible wellness experiences that balance academic excellence with emotional wellbeing.

Values

Care, clarity, community, and a professional commitment to sustainable success.

Why wellness in MBA life?

The MBA journey demands high performance and emotional agility. Our club integrates mindfulness, stress management, and leadership wellness so students can deliver their best work with clarity and calm.

Our story

Started by students, supported by faculty mentors, and driven by a shared commitment to holistic success.

2021 - Founding circle

Students launched a peer meditation circle to support exam-week resilience.

2022 - Leadership wellness labs

Workshops expanded into leadership coaching, emotional intelligence, and productivity science.

2023 - Campus wellness partnerships

Collaboration with SOMTU counselors brought evidence-based mental wellness programming.

2024 - Community engagement model

Peer facilitators trained to lead mindful leadership circles and wellbeing events.

Wellness Club

Wellness Club is a community dedicated to improving physical, mental, and emotional well-being through events, programs, and collaboration.
